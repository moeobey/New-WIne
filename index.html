About Us

New-Wine Innovation is birthed on the basic principles of merging varieties of organizations ranging from charitable to business organizations (mostly startups) to pull ideas collectively and achieve a global goal of influencing world activities through various merged start-ups and other existing organizations via our collective ideas.

Founded on May 2, 2018 by Daniel Adeniran, a student of Covenant University, New Wine Innovation likened to Warren Buffett’s Berkshire Hathaway is called an Innovation because its approach to winning organizations to be under the umbrella of the New Wine is the “Idea-barter” approach. This is simply making them sell-in through ideas, innovations and strategies to expanding their organizations via New Wine Innovation.
